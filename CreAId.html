Your always-ready assistant that feels like magic

This project presents a portable AI assistant application designed specifically for designers. It helps users manage their schedules, organize task priorities, and even monitor their health all within a single, integrated system. The goal of the application is to support designers in maintaining productivity and well-being by providing a smart, always-accessible assistant that adapts to their workflow and daily needs.

For those who live between reality and imagination.
