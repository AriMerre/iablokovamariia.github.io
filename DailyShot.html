DailyShot

(2025)

Drink that brightens everyday moments

This project focused on redesigning the DailyShot mobile application, a liquor shopping platform that allows users to pre-order alcoholic beverages online and pick them up at partnered offline stores.The main goal was to enhance UI consistency, usability, and brand identity. By emphasizing both visual clarity and functional convenience, the redesigned DailyShot app offers users a more intuitive and enjoyable way to access its core service — the “Liquor Pickup” system based on smart order and payment in advance.

A casual, easy, feel-good liquor pickup service for daily moments.
